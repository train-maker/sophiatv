## Principle

Do not clone brands, layouts, content, names, datasets, or proprietary features.

Clone the business pattern:

- one clear problem,
- instant answer,
- evergreen demand,
- fast mobile experience,
- SEO page that explains the tool,
- monetization only after traffic and compliance.

## Why This Fits Sophia

Sophia's app factory already builds small everyday tools. Utility sites are the web version of that same strategy.

Good utility-site traits:

- solves one task in seconds,
- requires no login,
- works on mobile,
- has repeat or share value,
- can be expanded into related tools,
- can support ads, affiliate links, lead gen, or digital products later.

## Build Rules

Each utility must ship with:

- original name and UI,
- local/offline-first function where possible,
- title and meta description,
- clear H1,
- short explanation,
- FAQ or use cases,
- no fake revenue claims,
- no copied merchant/product descriptions,
- no dark-pattern ads,
- no medical/legal/financial advice unless reviewed.

## SEO Selection Method

Pick ideas where:

- the user wants an instant answer,
- search intent is practical, not just curiosity,
- current top results are weak, dated, ugly, or not mobile-friendly,
- the tool can be meaningfully better than a generic calculator,
- content can add original value beyond the tool.

Avoid:

- crowded commodity calculators with strong incumbents,
- YMYL topics requiring expert review,
- copyrighted games or word databases copied from competitors,
- tools that need private user data to be useful.

## Monetization Ladder

1. No monetization until the page is useful.
2. Affiliate disclosure if recommending products.
3. `rel="sponsored nofollow"` on affiliate links.
4. Ads only after page speed and UX are acceptable.
5. AdSense, affiliate account creation, tax/payment details, or tracking pixels require Tarence approval.

## Standard Page Template

1. Tool surface above the fold.
2. One-sentence promise.
3. Inputs with sane defaults.
4. Result with copy/share/print.
5. Explanation of how calculation/checklist works.
6. "Who this is for / not for."
7. Optional product recommendations with disclosure.
8. FAQ.
9. Related tools.
