Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 24, 2026 · Last updated: April 24, 2026

SophiaMarket ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. It applies to sophiatv.vercel.app, the Sophia Connect app, and all related services.

1. Information we collect

You provide:

  • Account info: name, business name, email, phone, city, country
  • Listing content: business description, category, tags, logos, images
  • Payment info: processed directly by Square and/or Stripe — we do NOT store card numbers
  • Communications: messages you send through our platform

We collect automatically:

  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, clicks, search queries
  • Device: IP address, browser, OS, screen size
  • Cookies and similar technologies (see §5)
  • Analytics (Google Analytics 4, when enabled) — aggregated and anonymized

From third parties:

  • Payment processors (Square, Stripe): transaction status, payer info for subscription management
  • Authentication providers (if used): profile info you authorized them to share

2. How we use your information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Service
  • To display your Listing Content publicly (only what you submit)
  • To process payments, invoices, and subscription management
  • To respond to support requests
  • To send service-related emails (receipts, updates, security notices)
  • To send marketing emails only if you opt in — opt out any time via the email footer
  • To detect fraud, abuse, and enforce our Terms
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share only as follows:

  • Service providers: Vercel (hosting), Square/Stripe (payments), Google (analytics), Anthropic (AI features) — each bound by their own privacy terms
  • Publicly: Your Listing Content is publicly visible by design
  • Legal: When required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights and safety
  • Business transfers: In the event of merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with prior notice

4. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (GDPR for EU, CCPA for California, PIPEDA for Canada, etc.), you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Request a portable copy of your data
  • Object to processing or request restriction

To exercise these rights, email t.rain@sophiaoperator.com. We respond within 30 days.

5. Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential: login sessions, security — cannot be disabled
  • Analytics: aggregated usage data (Google Analytics 4) — opt out via browser controls or cookie banner
  • Preference: language, theme settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may break certain features.

6. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion, we remove most data within 30 days; we may retain limited records for legal, tax, or fraud-prevention purposes for up to 7 years.

7. Data security

We use industry-standard measures: HTTPS/TLS for all traffic, encryption at rest where applicable, access controls, and regular security reviews. No system is 100% secure; we will notify affected users within 72 hours of any confirmed breach of personal data.

8. International transfers

SophiaMarket operates from the United States. If you access the Service from outside the US, your data may be transferred to and processed in the US. By using the Service, you consent to such transfer. For EU/UK residents, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable.

9. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided information, email us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified by email (if we have yours) or a prominent notice on the site at least 14 days before taking effect.
