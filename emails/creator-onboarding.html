How to Go Live
1
Open Your Creator Dashboard
Log in at sophiaoperator.com/dashboard. From there, click "Go Live" in the top action bar.
2
Configure Your Stream
Enter a title, choose a category, and optionally set a pay-per-view price for your private session. Toggle AI translation on to reach global audiences.
3
Copy Your RTMP Details
You'll get an RTMP URL and stream key. Paste these into OBS, Streamlabs, or any streaming software. Click "Start Stream" when ready.
4
Engage Your Audience
The live chat is AI-powered. Viewers can ask Claude questions about your content, get summaries, and interact in their native language.

💡 Pro Tip: Best Times to Stream

Our data shows peak viewer activity between 7–10 PM EST and 12–3 PM PST on weekdays. Schedule your streams during these windows for maximum reach.

Upload Videos
1
Go to Video Manager
From your dashboard, select "Videos" → "Upload New Video". Supported formats: MP4, MOV, MKV, AVI. Max file size: 50GB.
2
Add Title & Thumbnail
A compelling thumbnail increases clicks by up to 40%. Recommended size: 1280×720 (16:9). Use high-contrast text on a dark background to match the SophiaTV aesthetic.
3
Enable AI Transcription
Toggle "AI Transcription" to automatically generate captions and make your video searchable in 50+ languages. Transcription takes ~2 minutes per hour of content.
