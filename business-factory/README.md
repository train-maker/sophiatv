# Sophia Business Factory

Purpose: split Sophia-related money paths into folders so Codex, Claude, Cursor, and local models can keep building without mixing everything together.

## CEO Rule

Codex runs the critical path and final verification. Free/local support tools can help. No paid spend, account permissions, API keys, outreach, legal/tax/medical claims, or public deployment without Tarence approval.

## Business Categories

| Folder | Business line | First money path |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `01-sophia-flagship` | Sophia website/app/social/translation marketplace | Launch-quality product, waitlist, trust-building demos |
| `02-simple-app-factory` | Offline-first small apps from real complaints | Sell templates, local tools, paid upgrades later |
| `03-local-service-tools` | Tools for local businesses | One-page apps, PDF binders, CSV workflows |
| `04-digital-products` | Checklists, binders, templates, scripts | Gumroad/Etsy/Shopify later, local PDFs first |
| `05-marketplace-seo` | SophiaMarket country/category pages | Organic search, listings, future paid featuring |
| `06-automation-kits` | Small business workflow automations | Local scripts, Zapier/Make alternatives later |
| `07-content-and-leads` | Educational content and lead magnets | Trust, email list later, no spam |

## Active Priorities

1. Finish Sophia website and Sophia app to a premium, working local preview.
2. Keep building offline-first Simple App Factory tools that can become sellable products.
3. Convert every research scout into either a build ticket, a template, or a discarded idea with reason.
4. Keep paid/API/cloud ideas in approval-gated backlog files.

## Owner Gates

- No hidden billing, spam, scraped personal data, scams, fake claims, stolen IP, or gray-market tactics.
- No legal, tax, medical, HR, immigration, environmental, platform-approval, or compliance-certification claims.
- Prefer local-first prototypes with CSV/PDF/printable export before accounts, databases, payments, or messaging APIs.
