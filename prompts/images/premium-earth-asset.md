# Premium Earth Asset Prompt

Create a photorealistic Earth viewed from space as if captured by a high-powered orbital camera. The image should feel real, elegant, cinematic, and premium, not cartoon, not sci-fi fantasy, not over-saturated. Show visible continents, cloud systems, atmospheric rim light, deep black space, subtle city-light hints only if physically plausible. No text, no logos, no UI, no symbols. Composition should work as a rotating globe texture and a luxury hero visual. High detail, realistic materials, natural color, restrained contrast.

Avoid:

- cartoon style,
- fake neon glow,
- fantasy planets,
- low-resolution texture look,
- heavy lens flare,
- religious symbolism,
- text or watermarks.
