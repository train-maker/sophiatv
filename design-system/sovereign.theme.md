# Sovereign

A black-diamond and gold-cartography theme honoring the Mansa Musa lineage: wealth shown through restraint, not ornament. Bone-warm white over true black. Gold behaves like currency, not decoration.

## Color Palette

- **Page**: `#06070A` — true black, page background
- **Page 2**: `#0B0D12` — near black, raised surfaces
- **Page 3**: `#1a1d26` — card top in gradients
- **Gold**: `#C8A24A` — primary CTA, kickers, numerals on accent
- **Gold Soft**: `rgba(200,162,74,0.18)` — hairlines
- **Gold Rule**: `rgba(200,162,74,0.30)` — dividers
- **Gold Strong**: `rgba(200,162,74,0.55)` — hover and active states
- **Bone**: `#F5F1E6` — warm white, body text
- **Bone 2**: `rgba(245,241,230,0.65)` — secondary text
- **Bone 3**: `rgba(245,241,230,0.40)` — tertiary text
- **Ocean**: `#7BD0E6` — focus state only
- **Green**: `#6EBE8E` — free-trial badge only

Banned: purple, pink, cyan-neon, and red-live styling on launch surfaces.

## Typography

- **Display serif**: Fraunces, Cormorant Garamond, Georgia, serif
- **UI sans**: Inter, system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif
- **Numeral mono**: JetBrains Mono, IBM Plex Mono, ui-monospace, monospace

Tracked caps use UI sans at 500 weight, uppercase, and deliberate spacing.

## Motion Budget

- Ease: `cubic-bezier(0.2, 0.7, 0.2, 1)`
- Minimum duration: 600ms
- Maximum duration: 1200ms
- No keyframe loops except the globe rotation or explicit functional loaders

## Geometry

- Max border radius: 8px
- Cards: 4px
- Buttons and inputs: 2px
- Shadows: rainforest-cave depth, single direction, deep and soft
- Corner notch: 28px Egyptian-pyramid cut, top-right of every cartouche

## Best Used For

Sophia website, app, social, market, and owner-facing surfaces where being on-platform must signal importance.
