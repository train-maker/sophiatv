SophiaMarket is the global business directory that sits where Yellow Pages ends and Alibaba becomes unaffordable. If your business sells to buyers across borders — or buyers in your own country who'd otherwise never find you — this guide walks you through getting listed in the next 10 minutes.

The two tiers

  • Featured listing — $29.99/month — gold-bordered, top placement in search results, listed across every regional landing page, priority in the AI recommendations panel. Cancel anytime.

Step 1 — Pick your listing tier

Visit /market-submit and choose Free or Featured. Featured pre-selects the fields that convert better (logo upload, up to 5 tags, social links) and routes your submission through the Stripe checkout.

Step 2 — Fill the form

  1. Business name — exactly as you want it to appear. Include your main service if it's not obvious from the name (e.g., "Lagos Prime Properties — Real Estate").
  2. Country + City — these drive which landing page you appear on.
  3. Category — pick the closest fit. You can only pick one, so choose the category where buyers are most likely to search.
  4. Description — 2–3 sentences. Lead with what you do, followed by a specific differentiator (certifications, years exporting, MOQs, service radius).
  5. Contact email — this is where inquiry forms route. Use a real monitored inbox.
  6. Website (optional) — adds credibility and gives visitors a second destination.

Step 3 — Pay and confirm

Featured listings go through Stripe Checkout. $29.99/month, first month on us for the first 100 businesses in any country. Free listings skip this step entirely.

Step 4 — Verify and publish

A confirmation email lands in your inbox within minutes of submission. Featured listings publish immediately; Free listings are reviewed within 24 hours to filter spam.

What happens after you list

Two things start happening immediately:

  1. Your listing appears on your country's landing page — e.g., if you're in Nigeria, you show up at /market/country/nigeria. Google indexes these within a week.
  2. Qualified buyers contact you via the inquiry form — filtered through the SophiaAI panel to reduce spam. Your email stays private until you reply.

Why Featured is worth $29.99

The gold border + top placement doubles click-through in internal A/B tests. For businesses that close even 1 deal a quarter from SophiaMarket traffic, the $360/year Featured cost is a fraction of one customer's lifetime value. Compared to:
