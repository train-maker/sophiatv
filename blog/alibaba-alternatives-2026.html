1. SophiaMarket — the AI-translated 105-country directory

Pricing: $29.99/month Featured, Free basic tier.
Strengths: Widest country coverage in the category. AI-translates your listing into 50+ languages automatically. Country-specific landing pages rank in Google for "[industry] in [country]" queries. Simple monthly subscription — no annual lock-in.
Weaknesses: Newer platform (launched 2026). Fewer buyers than Alibaba today, but the directory is explicitly built to rank organically in every country's local search.
Best for: Businesses outside China, service businesses, and any wholesaler who wants global reach without the $3,000+ annual commitment.

2. Alibaba — the 800-pound gorilla

Pricing: Gold Supplier $3,499/year.
Strengths: Massive buyer traffic. Built-in RFQ system.
Weaknesses: Overwhelmingly Chinese suppliers, so if you're not Chinese your listing gets lost. Annual commitment. Heavy competition within each category.
Best for: Chinese manufacturers, factories, bulk buyers who specifically want Chinese suppliers.

3. Global Sources — the trade show publisher

Pricing: From $2,699/year.
Strengths: Curates verified Asian suppliers. Runs the Global Sources Trade Show.
Best for: Electronics, consumer goods, and fashion buyers sourcing from Asia.

4. Made-in-China.com — the Alibaba alternative for Chinese suppliers

Pricing: $1,800+/year.
Best for: Chinese manufacturers who want a cheaper Alibaba alternative.

5. TradeKey — Middle East + Asia focus

Pricing: $600–3,000/year.
Best for: Regional wholesale across the Middle East and Asia.

6. EC21 — Korean-backed Asian directory

Pricing: Free basic, $1,500 premium.
Best for: Small Korean and Asian suppliers.

7. DHgate — retail-scale cross-border

Pricing: Free to list, fee on each sale.
Best for: Small-batch cross-border e-commerce where you ship directly to consumers.

How to pick

Three questions narrow it fast:

  1. Where are you? If you're in China, Alibaba is the default. Outside China, SophiaMarket or regional plays win.
  2. What's your ticket size? If you sell in container loads, stay on Alibaba/Global Sources. If you sell $500–$50k services or wholesale, SophiaMarket converts better per dollar spent.
  3. What's your commitment tolerance? SophiaMarket is the only one with a real monthly option; everyone else wants annual.
