Listing Guidelines

SophiaMarket quality rules for business profiles

What Makes A Strong Listing

  • Use your real business or trading name.
  • Choose the country, city, and category buyers would actually search.
  • Explain what you sell or provide in plain language.
  • Include concrete details: minimum order quantity, service area, certifications, years in business, export regions, or delivery timing.
  • Use a monitored business email so inquiries do not go unanswered.

What We Do Not Accept

  • False claims, impersonation, or misleading locations.
  • Illegal goods or services.
  • Spam, keyword stuffing, or copied descriptions.
  • Listings that require buyers to pay outside normal business protections before basic verification.

Review Process

Free listings are reviewed before publication. Featured listings may go live faster, but they are still subject to removal if they violate these guidelines or the Terms of Service.
