Common questions

How does the 7-day refund work?

If Featured isn't right for you, email hello@sophiaoperator.com within 7 days of your first charge for a full refund. No questions asked.

Can I switch tiers later?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime from your dashboard. Charges prorate against your current cycle.

What does the dedicated AI agent actually do?

For Enterprise, Sophia configures an agent that screens inbound messages, asks qualifying questions in the buyer's language, and routes only ready leads to your inbox.

How are payments handled?

All payments run through Stripe. Sophia never sees or stores your card details.

Is Free really free?

Yes. The Free tier is permanent. You only pay if you choose to upgrade for placement, multi-country listings, or the AI agent.

What's the difference between marketplace plans and creator plans?

Marketplace plans (above) are for businesses listing in the directory. Creator & Operator plans are for people who publish video, host sessions, or watch Sophia.
