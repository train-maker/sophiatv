Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel from your account settings with no penalties or lock-in periods.
What is the AI page management on Founder Tier?
Claude AI automatically writes your video descriptions, suggests tags, schedules posts, and drafts replies to comments — so you can focus entirely on creating.
What are Private Sessions?
1-on-1 or small group video calls with creators, powered by Agora. Creator Pro and Founder members can offer and book sessions directly on the platform.
How does the 50+ language translation work?
SophiaTV uses real-time AI translation to translate video titles, descriptions, chat messages, and comments into your preferred language automatically.
Is the Founder Tier price locked?
Yes. Founder Tier members who subscribe now lock in $49.99/month for life, even as we add more features and the price increases for new members.
What payment methods are accepted?
All major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers are also supported depending on your region.
