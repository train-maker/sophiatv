Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. All plans are month-to-month. Cancel from your account settings with no penalties or lock-in periods.
What is managed page setup on Founder Tier?
Founder support helps prepare page copy, listing structure, publishing checklists, and setup notes. Any external AI account stays optional and under the user's own control.
What are Private Sessions?
1-on-1 or small group access sessions for builders and operators. Creator Pro and Founder members can offer and book sessions directly on the platform once availability is finalized.
How does the 50+ language translation work?
Sophia includes a launch-safe translator with local fallback examples. People can connect their own translation provider later on their own terms.
How does the free first 30 days work?
Launch members can use paid-plan features free for the first 30 days after launch so they can test Sophia and share feedback. After the trial, the normal monthly rate shown on this page applies unless cancelled.
What payment methods are accepted?
Payments open after access review and production payment settings are confirmed.
